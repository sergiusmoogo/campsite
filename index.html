Welcome to Friends of Warthog

We are committed to empowering pastoral communities, preserving cultural heritage, and combating climate change for a brighter future.

About Us

Friends of Warthog was established in 2022 in Nasuroi village, Isiolo County, Kenya. Our mission is to empower pastoral communities through support for youth development, peace-building initiatives, cultural practices, and climate change mitigation.

Mission Statement

Empowering pastoral communities through support to youth development, peace-building initiatives, promotion and protection of Turkana cultural practices, mitigation of climate change, and community sensitization.

Vision for the Future

A safe, vibrant, and sustainable pastoralist community empowered as peace actors, active in cultural heritage preservation, and resilient to climate change.

Our Programs

  • Peace-building workshops and initiatives
  • Youth development and empowerment programs
  • Promotion and showcasing of Turkana cultural practices and heritage
  • Climate change awareness campaigns and interventions
  • Community sensitization and capacity-building initiatives
